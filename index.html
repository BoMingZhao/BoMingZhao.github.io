About Me

I am a Ph.D. student at the ZJU3DV group of State Key Lab of CAD&CG, Zhejiang University, supervised by Prof. Hujun Bao and Prof. Zhaopeng Cui. Before that, I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science at Beihang University, supervised by Prof. Yongxin Tong. Currently, I'm serving as an academic guest at ETH Zurich (Nov 2024 - Nov 2025), working with Prof. Marc Pollefeys. I have a broad interest in 3D vision technologies, including neural rendering, visual localization and 3D scene editing.
